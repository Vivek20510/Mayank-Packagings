Who We Are

Established in 2010, Mayank Packaging has grown from a small startup into a leading supplier of high-quality packaging solutions. Our team of dedicated professionals ensures that each product we offer meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. We specialize in providing a comprehensive range of packaging materials, including Leno Bags for agricultural produce, PP Bags for industrial applications, and BOPP materials for consumer goods.

What We Do

  • Leno Bags: Ideal for agricultural produce like onions, potatoes, and other vegetables, our Leno Bags are lightweight, breathable, and durable.
  • PP Bags: Versatile and strong, our PP Bags are suitable for carrying heavy materials such as cement, fertilizers, and chemicals.
  • BOPP Bags: Offering excellent print quality and moisture resistance, BOPP Bags are perfect for packaging consumer goods like food grains, animal feed, and more.
  • Customized Solutions: We provide tailor-made packaging solutions to match the specific needs and branding requirements of our clients.
